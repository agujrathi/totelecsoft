How long does it take to build a website?

We require approximately 30 days for developing and designing a website approximately 30 days to develop (online store) websites E-commerce. But the time may vary according to customer's requirement.

Who will I work with during the project?

We have experienced and skilled people's team to work on every project. You will be working with the concerned person working on your website.

Do I own my website?

YES! Everything that we design and develop will be 100% owned by you. Most of clients stay with us for the life of the website. Our team is just irresistible that way. But if for any reason you decide you want to take your site to another hosting service and get another company to service the site, we will happily assist you in making the transition as effortlessly and efficiently as possible. The website is yours after all, and we want you to take it wherever you go.

How much does web hosting cost?

The cost may vary according to your website.

Is a deposit required to start my project?

Yes. You have to provide us with 50% of the amount decided in advanced.

Can I modify my website design if I want?

Our designers make changes to the 'site design', while you control most of the 'site content'. This means you can modify images and text on your pages but you can't modify the overall layout of those pages.

Can you guarantee me a top ranking?

We don't guarantee that your website will reach a particular position on any search engine but we work on it diligently to give it a top ranking in any search engine.

How long will it take for my site to make it to the top of Google?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) work is a process that typically takes several months. The factors that influence the speed that your site advances in the rankings include the age of your site, the existing quality of the website and how it is optimized, your competition. We have helped clients with brand new websites rank on the first page of Google for competitive keywords within one to two months.

Which domain extension should I choose (.org, .net, .com, etc.)?

We usually try to get a domain that ends with .com since people will remember it. If there's no .com available for that name, We go with .net or .org.

Can I see how many visitors I have to my website?

Yes, you can. We can set up a free Google Analytics account for you, which will automatically record information about your website's visitors.

Do you register domain names?

No, we don't. We'll be more than happy to give you some advice on what your URL should be and give you some recommendations for where you can register a new domain.

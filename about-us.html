About TOTELEC

Totelec is Pune based IT service provider organization started in mid-2013, and is ISO 9001:2008 certified in January 2014. Totelec is achieving the real success of business in website design and development, software development, application development, E-commerce development and much more.

Totelec has catered 300+ customers and count is increasing significantly. We understand the requirements and business objectives and recommend a cost effective solution with latest technologies within the deadline. We follow Google standards, W3C standards.

We follow the classic software development lifecycle like the waterfall methodology. We start with the requirement and analysis phase followed by design and implementation phase and then the testing phase is followed to mark the completion of the product in terms of validating and verifying the quality.
